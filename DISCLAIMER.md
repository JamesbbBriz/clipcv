# clipcv — User Responsibility Disclaimer

By installing and using clipcv, you acknowledge and agree to the following.

## What clipcv is

clipcv is a browser extension that converts a single web page you are
currently viewing into a structured PDF or DOCX file using a Vision LLM
API key you configure yourself. The extension processes data locally in
your browser and sends it directly to the LLM provider you chose. No
clipcv-operated server is involved.

## What clipcv is not

clipcv is **not**:

- A web scraper. Each capture is a single-page, single-click, user-initiated
  action. There is no batch mode, no automated crawl, no scheduled run, no
  headless mode, no scripted trigger.
- A circumvention tool. clipcv does not bypass authentication, paywalls, or
  bot-detection measures.
- A redistribution platform. The output goes to your local machine.
  clipcv does not store, transmit, or republish data on any server
  controlled by the project.

## Your responsibilities

When you use clipcv, **you** are responsible for:

1. **Terms of service compliance** — Each website has its own terms
   governing what you may extract from pages you view. Many websites
   prohibit some or all forms of automated extraction even from pages you
   have legitimate access to. Before using clipcv on a site, read that
   site's terms of service and confirm your use is permitted. If in doubt,
   do not use clipcv there.

2. **Authorized access only** — Use clipcv solely on pages you legitimately
   have access to under your own authorized account.

3. **Data protection laws** — Comply with applicable laws in your
   jurisdiction (GDPR, PIPL, PDPA, CCPA, and others) for any personal data
   that ends up in files generated by clipcv.

4. **No bulk or automated use** — Do not script, automate, or otherwise
   bypass the manual single-click trigger.

5. **Your LLM provider's terms** — Captures are processed by the LLM
   provider you configure. Comply with their content policies.
